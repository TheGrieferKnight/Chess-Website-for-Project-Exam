Mastering Chess Tactics: How to Use Double Attacks to Your Advantage

How to Create Powerful Double Attacks and Win Material on the Board

By: Fabian Steineck

February 28, 2023

chess strategy games chess tactics

There are several types of double attacks in chess, including fork attacks, discovered attacks, and pin attacks. Fork attacks involve attacking two pieces at once with a single move, while discovered attacks involve moving a piece to reveal an attack from another piece behind it. Pin attacks involve attacking a valuable piece that is protecting another less valuable piece, forcing the opponent to choose which piece to save.

To use double attacks effectively, players must be able to identify potential targets and plan their moves accordingly. They must also be aware of the positions of their own pieces and the opponent's pieces, as well as the potential consequences of their moves.
